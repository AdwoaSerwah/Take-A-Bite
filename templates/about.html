About Take-A-Bite

Take-A-Bite was born out of a passion for making food delivery and pick-up more convenient in Accra. What makes Take-A-Bite special is our focus on simplicity, and user experience.

Whether you're grabbing lunch or quick snack, Take-A-Bite ensures that your food arrives hot, fresh, and on time. This project is not only a practical tool but also a portfolio project developed during my time at ALX School.
